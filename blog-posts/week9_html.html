Blogging to Learn

October 20, 2014

In the past couple of months as I have worked through Phase 0 of Dev Bootcamp, I have been given several blog assignments. These are the blogposts that have populated my blog up until now.

By writing these reflections and explanations of what I have been learning, I have solidified my knowledge on the topics I write about.

As they say, the best way to test if you understand a concept is trying to teach it to somebody else. Many of my blogging assignments have been to do just that: Explain a concept to someone who does not have a technical background. I can definitely vouch for the effectiveness of the exercises. By writing these posts, I have a gained a deeper understanding of the material I've been learning.





Relatedly, my most recent assignment was to revamp my website and set up my own personal domain. I had a lot of fun completely redesigning the site, now that I have a little more knowledge than when I initially designed it. I also managed to snag my name as a domain, which I'm pretty excited about.
